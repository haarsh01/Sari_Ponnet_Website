About Me

Mijn Introductie

I am a hardworking, friendly individual with a high academic standing. I am the course representative for my Marine Biology with Oceanography degree at the university of Southampton where I work together with professors and staff from the university being an essential link between students and staff. I am currently responsible for the social media and webpages for the charity Sea Sanctuaries Trust and I am part of the Marine Plastic Special Interest Group (SIG) for IMarEST. Last June, I was a Science Officer on board of a sailing ship doing plastic pollution surveys, cetacean and bird watches on board whist also doing sail training. I was a Research Assistant in the marine botany lab at UNCW for 1 semester.
